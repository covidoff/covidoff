How to use the View Data Portal

Welcome to Covidoff View Data Portal. Follow the Below Steps to View you required related Data. For the People. By the People. Off the People.

Choose your Requirement from the Dropdown Given Above.

There are Seven Options as Services. Plasma Requirements, Plasma Donors. Oxygen Requirements. Oxygen Donors. Bed Availability. Remdesivr Availability, Other Resources.

Choose either 'View Full Data' or 'View Filtered Data' (Filtered by Ditrict)

Choose View Full Data to see all the Datas that are available there related to that particular service in our Database and choose view filtered data to sort data by District (or Blood Group in case of Plasma Requirements or Donors).

If you Chose 'View Filtered Data', Select Ditrict from Drop Down.

If you want to find Data from West Bengal's Districts then there is a direct option to selcet a West Bengal Distrcit but in other cases, you have to choose 'Not Listed' at the end of the Drop Down, then enter your Distrcit name, if it matches with any entries of our database, it will be showed accordingly.

FAQ: "I couldn't find a leads District outside West Bengal, that was previously there."

If there are any conflicted leads that are reported to us or any lead requirement that is fullfilled and reported to us, we remove that from our database. You can see the full database choosing the View Full Data option to get a better clarity
